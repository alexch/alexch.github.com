Sure! So is restraint! And so is moderation! And so is, um, pruning (to use a bad metaphor for shrinkage).

The point is that slogans frame the debate allowing our poor little metaphor-addicted brains to deal with detailed and subtle policy positions. Here the left- and even center-leaning economists and wonks and CEOs are all saying we have too little demand, but the debate was between different ways and amounts of reducing demand (via reducing government spending), not about how or even whether to increase it. Which is a testament to the Republicans' success at messaging, but is also pretty fucked up. Aug 25, 2011 Aug 25, 2011
