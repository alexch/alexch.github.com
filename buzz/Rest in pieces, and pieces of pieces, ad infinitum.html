My first big programming "moment" was writing a mandelbrot set program on my TI-85. I kicked off the code and just had a blank screen - no feedback at all. The next day I came back to a blank screen and figured I did something wrong...I hit the power button and for about half a second a fully formed mandelbrot set was there in all its glory, before the power shut off and I had to get new batteries and the image was lost.

Very dramatic. And one of those times when the drama you correctly perceive is completely different from what anyone else will register when you recount the story. Oct 18, 2010 Oct 18, 2010
