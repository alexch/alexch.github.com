Until I see a mockup or preview, I have to assume they'll fuck it up.

The genesis of Plus was an attempt to clone Facebook mixed with an overreaction to the privacy shitstorm of Buzz, which in turn was an attempt to clone Twitter. They have no idea how people use Reader -- it's a curated feed producer and aggregator and reader, with built in comment threads and friend discovery -- so I expect they have no idea how to migrate features to a totally different product with different goals and users.

Does Plus emit RSS per user? Does it emit an aggregate RSS feed of a given circle?

What about my "Awesome" list, containing the blogs and friends I know I must read, as opposed to the hundreds of others I subscribe to for when I have time? Oct 22, 2011 Oct 22, 2011
